A Brief Story About Me

When I was studying for a language degree, I found myself inclining towards a life pursuit of problem solving. I had also started to dabble in online trading where I was convinced that a systematic approach was crucial to avoiding falling prey to the irrationalities of the markets. That has made me aspire to get into the world of quantitative finance as a software engineer, and hopefully becoming a quant trader. However, realising that I wasn't a good autodidact, I have come to Xccelerate for a more organised, reliable approach to learning software development.

What Do I Expect to Get from the Bootcamp?

Given the speed of the technological changes we have witnessed recently, the best outcome from a bootcamp experience should be the ability of picking up any new knowledge according to the demands of the job. In other words, I am learning how to learn, which is exactly what I am shooting for; one is forced to adapt under pressure.
